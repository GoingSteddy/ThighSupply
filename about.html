Our mission

A short pair of shorts with a long set of receipts.

ThighSupply was founded in 2021 by two former pattern-makers who got tired of seeing the same compromises: cheap synthetics, opaque supply chains, and feel-good marketing that didn't translate into anything anyone could actually verify. We make one product family — short unisex shorts — and we make it carefully.

1-for-10, every month

Every ten pairs we sell triggers one pair donated. We tally sales monthly and ship cases of 50 to partnered shelter networks. The counter on our homepage reflects donations delivered — not ordered, not promised.

Current shelter partners: Coalition for the Homeless (NYC), Crisis (UK), Comunidade Vida e Paz (Lisbon), and five regional networks across the US Midwest and Pacific Northwest.

Where our materials come from

  • Hemp: Coltivare cooperative, Romagna, Italy. EU organic certified.
  • Cotton: Suminter India Organics, Madhya Pradesh. GOTS certified.
  • Recycled nylon: Aquafil ECONYL®, regenerated from ocean and landfill waste.
  • Dyes: bluesign®-approved, low-impact reactive dyes only.
  • Trims: Corozo nut buttons, recycled-polyester thread, OEKO-TEX elastics.

Where our shorts are made

Cut and sewn in two workshops:

  • Vila Nova de Famalicão, Portugal — 32 sewers, Fair Wear Foundation member, audited annually.
  • Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu, India — 78 sewers, Fair Trade USA certified, on-site childcare and health clinic.

We publish our full factory list, audit reports, and wage ladders every January. Read the 2025 transparency report →

What we don't do

  • No virgin polyester. Ever.
  • No PFAS or "forever chemical" finishes.
  • No air freight on regular orders.
  • No charity-of-the-month gimmicks. The 1-for-10 program is the program.
  • No new colorways every six weeks. We rotate the palette once a year.

Wear a pair, give a pair (eventually).

That's the deal. Simple math, real shorts, real donations.
